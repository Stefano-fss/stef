What's FreeSpace?

Do you have too much stuff to bring home for just a summer or a semester abroad? Try out FreeSpace! We are a platform that connects those who need space to nearby people who have it.

Never again will you have to drive to a storage facility or pay for more than what your items are worth. Instead, choose a host that will pick up, store, and deliver your stuff. We even offer free boxes with every booking. So, what are you waiting for? Free up your space now!

How FreeSpace Works?

Learn more

1. Hosts List Their Space

Hosts specify their rates and what items they are willing to store. Hosts are properly vetted through background checks.

2. Renters Browse Listings

Renters browse the listings to choose a host that fits their needs. Every booking comes with free boxes.

3. Hosts Store Stuff

Drop off your stuff or have it picked up for you. Hosts are guaranteed to keep your belongings safe.

4. Retrieve Your Items

When you need your stuff back, schedule a pick up time or have it dropped off for you.
